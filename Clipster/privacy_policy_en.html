Privacy Policy

Dear user, thank you for using Clipster.

Clipster respects and protects all of your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Clipster handles your information.

1. Information Collection

Clipster does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

  • Keyboard Extension: Used only to provide input functionality. It does not collect or upload your input content.
  • Clipboard Access: Used only for saving and managing copy records. It does not upload or store clipboard content.
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable this feature, data will only be stored in your iCloud account. Clipster does not access or save this data.

2. Information Usage

Since Clipster does not collect any information, it does not use or share your information.

3. User Rights

You have the following rights:

  • You may disable iCloud sync at any time.
  • You may delete local data through your device system settings.
  • You may delete or manage data stored in iCloud using Apple’s iCloud management tools.

4. Data Security and Retention

Your data is stored only locally on your device or in your iCloud account. It is not uploaded to the developer’s server. The retention period is under your control, and you may delete it at any time.

5. In-App Purchases

Clipster’s in-app purchase functionality is provided and processed by the Apple App Store. All transactions are completed through Apple’s secure system.

6. Data Security

Your settings and preferences are stored only locally on your device or in your iCloud account. They are not uploaded to the developer’s server. You may access, modify, or delete them at any time.

7. Policy Updates

If this Privacy Policy is updated, we will announce it within the application.
